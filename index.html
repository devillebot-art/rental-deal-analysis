🎯 Golden Rule for This Market

For every $50K you negotiate off the purchase price → ~$333/mo more cash flow.

To achieve 120% cash flow on a typical 2BR rental:

  • Purchase price ≈ $130K–$180K (depending on condition)
  • OR find properties where insurance is below average
  • OR find two units that generate combined rental income

💡 Key Insights

1. The Sanford Two-Units Deal is the Most Promising
Two units on one lot = stacking rental income. If both units are legally separate and can be financed on one loan, this could genuinely hit 120%+ cash flow.
2. Insurance is the #1 Cash Flow Killer
$700–$865/mo in insurance alone nearly equals a full mortgage payment. Non-negotiable in FL. Budget for it.
3. Deltona Has the Best Price Points
Deltona consistently shows properties under $200K, some under $170K. That's where the cash flow math works. Altamonte Springs and Lake Mary are overpriced for this strategy.
4. Price-to-Rent Ratio Target
  • Good: < 12 (buy ratio) → cash flow viable
  • Typical in corridor: 15–18 → marginal to negative
  • Your target with 120% buffer: ratio < 10 required
